Pengfei Li

CS Ph.D student
Huazhong University of Science and Technology

 


Biography:Pengfei Li is a Ph.D student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Yu Hua. He obtained the B.E degree in Computer Science from HUST in 2017.

His research interests include computer systems and memory architecture, including in-memory index techniques, deduplication, and non-volatile memory systems.

[VLDB] Pengfei Li, Yu Hua, Jingnan Jia, Pengfei Zuo, “FINEdex: A Fine-grained Learned Index Scheme for Scalable and Concurrent Memory Systems”, Proceedings of the 48th International Conference on Very Large Data Bases (VLDB), 2022.

[Middleware] Pengfei Li, Yu Hua, Qin Cao, Mingxuan Zhang, “Improving the Restore Performance via Physical-Locality Middleware for Backup Systems”, Proceedings of the annual ACM/IFIP Middleware conference (Middleware), 2020.
